# Coffee Cafe - Your Ultimate Coffee Experience

**Coffee Cafe** is a modern and user-friendly website designed for coffee enthusiasts. Whether you're a fan of rich espressos, creamy lattes, or flavored cold brews, Coffee Cafe offers everything you need to explore the world of coffee. The site provides a clean and interactive interface to browse various coffee types, learn more about the coffee culture, and discover brewing methods.

## Features:
- **Home Page**: Welcome to Coffee Cafe, showcasing popular coffee drinks, new arrivals, and a brief introduction to the coffee world.
- **Menu Page**: Detailed descriptions of all the coffee types available in the cafe, along with images and descriptions.
- **About Us Page**: A section to learn about the history of Coffee Cafe, our mission, and how we source the finest beans.
- **Contact Us Page**: A contact form for inquiries, feedback, and to connect with the Coffee Cafe team.

## Technology Stack:
- **HTML**: Used for structuring the content and layout of the website.
- **CSS**: Provides styling for the website, ensuring it is responsive and looks good on all screen sizes.
- **JavaScript**: Adds interactive features like sliders, buttons, and dynamic content updates.
- **GitHub Pages**: Hosting the website live for easy access by users.
